About:


Tony's Produce Market is the long-awaited dream of Tony Pernell Waldron. Tony, tired of turning terrain in tonkas, takes to tending a tienda in 2015. Pernell, as his loved ones like to call him, is dedicated to providing the freshest produce of the utmost quality with the highest levels of attention to his valued customers. Pernell sources the majority of his quality produce from The Jacksonville Produce Emporium. Pernell also loves Jesus. Jesus of Nazareth. Not Jesus Navas.
