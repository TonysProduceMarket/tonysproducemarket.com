Tony's Story:


The best things in life are worth waiting for... That’s what I’ve always been told, and I suppose there is truth in that. I am a native Floridian and have worked on a family farm much of my life in central Florida. Mostly because it was a way to make a living; however, I still found satisfaction in it. It has been several years since I last farmed "full-time", but I've managed to plant something in a patch of dirt nearly every year since then. I guess you could say that I have dirt in my veins.

I get fired up when you start talking about produce. I love those road-side, pop-up markets (you know, the here today gone tomorrow ones). I have always wanted my own produce market. Fresh is best, and the fresher, the better. My goal is to carry as much locally-grown produce as I can. You have my word that everything you find in my market will be as fresh as it can possibly be. I select the best for you and your family, just as I would select for me and mine.

We will be here today, and tomorrow to provide the freshest fruits and vegetables for you.
